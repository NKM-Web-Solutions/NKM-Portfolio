Case Study

Llama.app

A fresh, new take on an existing website for brand ambassadors to join the Llama.app Shopify marketing platform.

Problem

The pre-existing Llama.app Ambassadors website had experienced a significant decline in signups for the Llama.app marketing platform. Recognizing the critical importance of effectively engaging this particular audience segment, the client sought a comprehensive overhaul of the website's design. With the overarching goal of bolstering sign-up rates for the platform, the client desired a brand new design that would resonate more deeply with Ambassadors and inspire increased engagement.

Solution

To create a bolder, brighter and more modernized website with real-life screenshots from the Llama.app marketing platform to reach the target audience of brand ambassadors and engage them to sign up. Additionally, the new website would highlight the array of features offered by the platform, emphasizing their value proposition outline features for brand ambassadors and how they can increase their revenue. The new website would also have significantly more opportunities to sign up increasing the likelihood of the brand ambassadors signing up exponentially.
